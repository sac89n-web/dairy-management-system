Railway PostgreSQL Schema Setup

1. Access Railway PostgreSQL

In your Railway dashboard:

  • Go to your PostgreSQL service
  • Click on "Query" tab
  • Copy and paste the complete script from Scripts/railway-complete-schema.sql
  • Click "Execute"
